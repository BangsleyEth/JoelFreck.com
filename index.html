About

Joel
Freck

Canadian Producer/DJ from Halifax. Over the past few years Joel has gained support from Armin Van Buuren, Above & Beyond, Laidback Luke, and Fedde Le Grand, among others.

In 2018 he won the Anjunabeats producer contest hosted by Beatport, earning a signing and a spot on Anjunabeats Worldwide. Shortly after, he took first place in Laidback Luke's 'Rockin' with the Best' remix competition — sharing the stage with Luke in San Diego and Las Vegas.

In 2020, his collaboration 'Dawn' was selected for Armada's A State of Trance label, appearing on Armin's Ibiza 2020 compilation and ASOT Year Mix. Back-to-back ECMA nominations for Dance Recording of the Year followed in 2021 and 2022.
